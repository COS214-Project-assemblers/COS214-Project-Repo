\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a customer with some plant experience who can handle moderate care requirements. 

This customer type prefers plants with \char`\"{}\+Moderate\char`\"{} care level but will accept \char`\"{}\+Easy\char`\"{} plants as fallback. Inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classCustomer}{Customer}} base class and implements the specific dialogue patterns and plant preferences.
