\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Declares manager class which is responsible for managing the creation of plant offers, and dealing with customers through the sales floor. 

The \mbox{\hyperlink{classManager}{Manager}} class coordinates the interactions between the sales floor and customers. It builds \mbox{\hyperlink{classPlant}{Plant}} offers for the customers, and processes the transactions (recorded/lost)
