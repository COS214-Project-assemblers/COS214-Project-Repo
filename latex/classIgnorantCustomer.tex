\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Represents a customer with minimal plant knowledge who needs easy-\/care plants. 

This customer type prefers plants that are hard to kill and require minimal maintenance. They have simple dialogue patterns reflecting their lack of plant knowledge and seek plants that can survive with minimal attention.

Inherits from \mbox{\hyperlink{classCustomer}{Customer}} base class and implements the ignorant customer type with specific dialogue patterns and plant preferences.
